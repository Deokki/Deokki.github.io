About Room for Rent Company

Room for Rent Company was established on February 2035 with our Founder and Ceo Ikki Dominique Modar, along with our staffs. It was once started as a dream of a young woman to have a stable life and to help others in a way of renting a room. No matter what their financial stability in their life. Through years of planning and investing. It is where our company rises from a single dream along with the motto 'The light is what guides you home, the warmth is what keeps you there' by Ellie Rodriguez
