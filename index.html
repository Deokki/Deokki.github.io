Room to Rent Co.

Room for Rent Company is a friendly money saver renting place, wherein we provide you a room to rent in a very ecofriendly and money saver price. It is located nearly to the daily needs market or shop of our tenant. Even their workplace.

We established this kind of business for the benefits of others. Who are not financially stable or having hard time looking for a renting place they can call home

In this one room aparment we have a plan's average size ranges from 800 - 1500 sq ft with 1 bedroom, 1 kitchen, 1 sala, 1 bathroom and a terrace area. Not only a space friendly to our tenants but it is aesthetically beautiful to match their vibes

About Room for Rent Company

Room for Rent Company was established on February 2035 with our Founder and Ceo Ikki Dominique Modar, along with our staffs. It was once started as a dream of a young woman to have a stable life and to help others in a way of renting a room. No matter what their financial stability in their life. Through years of planning and investing. It is where our company rises from a single dream along with the motto 'The light is what guides you home, the warmth is what keeps you there' by Ellie Rodriguez
